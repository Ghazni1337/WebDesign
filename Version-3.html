Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What websites can you scrape data from?

We can extract data from virtually any website, including e-commerce platforms (Amazon, eBay, AliExpress, Walmart), directories (Google Maps, Yelp, TripAdvisor), and custom websites. Our advanced systems can handle both static and dynamic (JavaScript-heavy) websites, even those with anti-scraping protections.

Is web scraping legal?

Web scraping is legal when done ethically and in compliance with website terms of service. We strictly adhere to robots.txt files and implement rate limiting to avoid overloading servers. We never scrape personal data or copyrighted content without permission.

How do you handle CAPTCHAs and anti-bot measures?

We employ multiple strategies including CAPTCHA-solving services, IP rotation, headless browsers with human-like behavior patterns, and other proprietary techniques to bypass anti-scraping measures while maintaining ethical standards. Our success rate exceeds 98% for even the most protected sites.

What formats can you deliver the data in?

We provide data in any format you need: CSV, Excel (XLS/XLSX), JSON, XML, or direct API integration with your Shopify, WooCommerce, or custom database. We can also set up automated pipelines to deliver fresh data on your preferred schedule (daily, weekly, real-time).

How does the milestone payment system work?

Projects are divided into 3-4 clear milestones. You only pay for a milestone after approving the delivered work. For example: 1) Initial data sample (20% payment), 2) Full dataset extraction (50% payment), 3) Final formatted delivery (30% payment). This ensures zero risk for you.
